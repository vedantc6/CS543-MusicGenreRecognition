Genre Belief Recognition using Convolutional Recurrent Neural Networks




This website acts as a demo for the CS543: Massive Data Storage and Retrieval final project on music genre recognition through audio samples.
We have used a CRNN end-to-end model to predict the genre belief in a song. The audio samples can be uploaded by the button given below.
As the audio progresses, a visualization will be shown in terms of a Pie Chart, which will show what our network believes the genre to be at any point of time.

Please wait while your song is being processed. Processing depends on the length of the song and you may have to wait for a few minutes if the file is large.
